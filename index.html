Privacy Policy for Randomdex

Welcome to Randomdex! This Privacy Policy outlines how the information is handled by the app.

Information Collection

Randomdex does not collect any personal information from its users. The app only accesses the Internet to retrieve data from the PokeAPI. This data includes information about Pokémon and is used solely for displaying it within the app.

Information Usage

The data retrieved from the PokeAPI is used exclusively for the purpose of displaying random Pokémon details within the Randomdex app. The app does not collect, store, or share any user data with third parties.

Internet Permissions

The app requests Internet permission solely to make calls to the PokeAPI and retrieve Pokémon data. No personal information is transmitted or stored during this process.

Fair Use of PokeAPI

Randomdex utilizes data from the PokeAPI in accordance with its fair use policy. You can review PokeAPI's fair use policy here.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I reserve the right to update or modify my Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of the app. By continuing to use Randomdex, you acknowledge and agree to the updated Privacy Policy.
